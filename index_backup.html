About The White Meat Company

Pioneering sustainable rabbit farming and premium white meat dining in Zimbabwe's capital city.

Our Story

Founded in Belvedere, Harare, The White Meat Company emerged from a vision to revolutionize Zimbabwe's approach to sustainable protein farming while promoting healthy white meat alternatives.

We combine traditional farming wisdom with modern techniques to deliver premium rabbit breeding services, top-quality equipment, and comprehensive training through our Rabbit Farming Masterclass program.

500+ satisfied customers
50+ masterclass graduates

Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

To lead Zimbabwe's sustainable rabbit farming revolution while providing exceptional white meat dining experiences that promote healthy living and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To become the premier destination for rabbit farming excellence and white meat cuisine across Southern Africa, setting the standard for quality, sustainability, and innovation.

Essential Rabbit Farming Tips

Housing

Provide adequate space, ventilation, and protection from weather conditions.

Nutrition

Balanced diet with quality pellets, fresh vegetables, and unlimited clean water.

Health Care

Regular health checks, vaccinations, and immediate treatment of any issues.

Records

Keep detailed records of breeding, health, and production for better management.
